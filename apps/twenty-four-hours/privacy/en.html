Privacy Policy

Twenty Four Hours
Last Updated: January 26, 2025

Willee Project ("Developer") values the privacy of Twenty Four Hours ("App") users and complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Required Information

No information is collected for basic app functionality. Schedule data is stored only on your device.

1.2 Optional Information

Item Purpose Collection Timing
Google Account (email) Cloud sync service When user enables sync
Advertising ID Personalized advertising When ads are displayed
Purchase Information In-app purchase processing and ad removal When making a purchase

2. Data Storage

2.1 Local Storage

Schedule data, completion records, and app settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to the developer's servers.

2.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync, your data is stored in your own Google Drive. The developer cannot access this data.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Advertising (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Billing: In-app purchase processing
  • Google Drive API: Cloud sync (optional)
  • Google Sign-In: Account authentication (optional)

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local data: Automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.
  • Cloud data: You can delete it directly from Google Drive.
  • Advertising ID: Can be reset or disabled in device settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Choose whether to use cloud sync
  • Remove ads through in-app purchase
  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the app
  • Disconnect your Google account

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time.

7.1 Delete Local Data

All data stored on your device (schedules, completion records, settings) will be automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

  • Android: Settings → Apps → Twenty Four Hours → Uninstall
  • Windows: Delete the app folder or remove via Control Panel

7.2 Delete Cloud Data

To delete backup data stored in Google Drive:

  • Go to Google Drive
  • Navigate to "My Drive" → App data folder and delete "twenty_four_hours_backup.json.gz"
  • Or disconnect the app from Google Account Permissions

7.3 Disconnect Google Account

To disconnect your Google account from the app:

  • In-app Settings → Cloud Sync → Sign Out
  • Or remove "Twenty Four Hours" access from Google Account Permissions

7.4 Data Deletion Request

If you have difficulty deleting data using the above methods, please contact us and we will assist you.

8. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated due to legal requirements or service changes. We will notify you of significant changes through in-app notifications.
